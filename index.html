I am currently a visiting student of IVUL at KAUST, supervised by Prof. Bernard Ghanem.

Before that I was a research intern at UISEE, a Chinese start-up AI-Driving company. I focus on real-time object detection using LiDAR point clouds, mentored by Yang Wen. During my intern, I have combined VoxelNet with our own data set. I adjusted the parameters of the networks and did data augmentation to improve recall rate. For classical networks of 2D object detection, such as R-CNN and YOLO series, I have a full understanding of theory and code implementation. Meanwhile, I am skilled at using C/C++ and Python to complete project development and model implementation. I am also familar with open source libraries like Numpy, PCL and OpenCV.

During my graduate study, I visited NUS and worked on remote monitoring and control of UAV, advised by Prof. Colin TAN. I obtained my bachelor degree from Electrical Engineering Department, Xi'an Jiaotong University.
